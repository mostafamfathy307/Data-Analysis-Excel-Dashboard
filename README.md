# 📈 Marketing Campaign Performance Report

### 📌 Executive Summary
The marketing campaign demonstrated strong performance in **reach, engagement, and revenue generation**.  
- **Impressions:** 14.6M  
- **Conversion Value:** 1.7M SAR  
- **Spend:** 163K SAR  
- **ROI:** High  

---

### 📊 Performance Highlights
- **14.6M Impressions:** Wide reach across channels.  
- **119K Shares:** High content engagement.  
- **1.7M SAR Conversion Value:** Strong revenue impact.  
- **163K SAR Spend:** Efficient budget utilization.  

---

### 📍 Channel Insights
- **Instagram:** Highest revenue (684.8K SAR) and CTR (46.83%). Best for audience engagement.  
- **Snapchat:** Best ROI (28.2K SAR spend, 634.7K SAR revenue).  
- **Facebook:** Needs optimization (Highest spend 71.6K SAR, lowest revenue). Improve targeting and messaging.  

---

### 🌍 Geographical Performance
- **Riyadh:** Highest reach and revenue. Focus budget here.  
- **Dammam:** Second highest performance.  
- **Jeddah:** Opportunities remain.  

---

### 🍂 Seasonal Insights
- **Autumn campaign** had the highest conversions.  
- **Instagram and Snapchat** showed notable growth in September and October.  

---

### 💡 Strategic Recommendations
- Increase investment in **Instagram and Snapchat**.  
- Improve **Facebook performance** before scaling spend.  
- Focus budget on **Riyadh** for maximum ROI.  
- Implement advanced **retargeting** and refine messaging to increase conversions.  

---

### 📌 Conclusion
The campaign showed **high success in engagement and ROI**. Tactical improvements, especially in Facebook, and expansion in high-performing channels can yield even better results in upcoming high-conversion seasons.
